How do I approach a project

Is this a problem of usability, navigation, or customer satisfaction? With a qualitative background, it is important to understand users' needs and motivations. This can be done through interviews or focus groups. If it is a qualitative issue, we could measure satisfaction through surveys, and data analysis

Resources and limitations

Do we have a big research team, do we have enough time and budget? With a big team, we can conduct exhaustive studies, but if we don't, a micro survey could do the job.

Context

What is the stage in the development cycle of the product?

Users

Are they experts in the field, what is their tech literacy level? What do they need? We can answer these questions through usability tests with them, empathy maps, etc.

Flexibility

A mix of various methods or approaches could work, this is the perfect time to get creative!
